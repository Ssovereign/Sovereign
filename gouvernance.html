Governance

The main purposes of Sovereign DAO Tokens are to incentivise liquidity providers on the Sovereign DAO platform as well as getting as many users as possible involved in the governance of the protocol involved. Currently SOV has two main uses: voting and staking. Staking will allow you to earn fees collected across the different products Sovereign DAO is offering.
